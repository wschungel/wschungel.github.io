Privacy Policy — BeachCam

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the BeachCam app developed by 12-bit Games (Schungel BV).

Information We Collect

BeachCam requests access to your device's location. This is used solely to show webcams near your current location. Your location data is not stored, not shared with third parties, and is only used within the app during your session.

Advertising

BeachCam displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

BeachCam is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

BeachCam does not store any personal data on our servers. Location data is only used locally on your device and is not retained after closing the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact
